# Cafe Jinny — A Cozy Hidden Gem in Gangdong-gu, Seoul

This isn't one of those places you stumble upon by accident. It's tucked inside an alley — you have to know it's there. But once you step in, you immediately think, "Oh, this place is nice."

34 Cheonjung-ro 43-gil, Gangdong-gu, Seoul, 1st floor. **Cafe Jinny**.

## First Impression — Stainless Steel Meets Warm Wood

![Cafe Jinny counter](../../assets/cafe-jinny-counter.jpg)

The first thing you notice is the clean stainless steel counter. It could feel cold, but the wooden countertop and warm lighting strike a perfect balance. Digital menu boards line the wall above, showing a solid range — coffee, smoothies, fruit ades, herb teas.

The cafe itself isn't huge, but that's part of the charm. It has that cozy neighborhood cafe feeling you can't fake.

## The Space — Solo or Group, Both Work

![Cafe Jinny interior seating](../../assets/cafe-jinny-interior.jpg)

Walk further in and you'll find a large communal wood table. The walls are lined with cedar-toned panels, a whale painting hanging above. A shelf with plants and books, fresh flower arrangements on the table — it's curated but never forced.

The layout works for small group gatherings too. Power outlets are available, so laptop workers are clearly welcome.

## The Latte — Where the Real Skill Shows

![Heart latte art](../../assets/cafe-jinny-latte.jpg)

A cafe latte served in a pink mug with a clean heart-shaped latte art. Pretty, yes — but the real test is the taste. The milk-to-espresso ratio is spot on. Not bitter, not bland. Just right.

If you've been drinking nothing but chain cafe lattes, this is the moment you remember what a properly pulled latte tastes like.

## Little Details That Matter

![Cafe Jinny sign](../../assets/cafe-jinny-sign.jpg)

A cute illustrated sign on the wall reads: "One drink per person is love ♡" — not the typical stern notice, but something that matches the cafe's personality. "We'll repay you with even better drinks" adds a touch of sincerity from the owner.

These small details add up to make you think, "I'll definitely come back."

## How to Get There

- 📍 **Address**: 34 Cheonjung-ro 43-gil, Gangdong-gu, Seoul, 1F #104
- 🗺️ **[Naver Map](https://naver.me/x2hv6k2C)**
- 🚇 **Line 5, Gildong Station** — Exit 2 or 3, walk toward Gupeundari Station. Pass Starbucks, turn left into the alley next to Olbareun Seoul Hospital (~5 min walk)
- 🚇 **Line 5, Gupeundari Station** — Head toward Gangdong-gu Community Center Exit 1, turn right at the alley before Olbareun Seoul Hospital
- 🅿️ By car: search "Cafe Jinny" or the address. It's inside an alley so it's easy to miss — look for the block behind Olbareun Seoul Hospital

## Bottom Line

Nothing flashy — just a great latte, warm atmosphere, and a quiet corner of Gangdong-gu. Worth a detour if you're anywhere nearby.
